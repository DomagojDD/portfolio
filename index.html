About Me



My name is Domagoj Dugi and I live in Varazdin, Croatia. I'm Photographer, Videographer, Designer, Frontend Developer and more or less everything related to multimedia. I always try to combine my knowledge and skills to make my projects better and more successful.

When I enrolled in high school for a media technician, I'm gained a lot of knowledge and experience about photography, video and television. Also, I was participated in projects Comenius and ACES for which ones I have been in Turkey and North Macedonia. There I met other cultures and gained new acquaintances.

After that, I enrolled at the University North on the department of Multimedia, Design and Application which I'm still attending. Here I have further improved my knowledge and gained some new skills such as design, programming and marketing.

For myself, I would say that I am responsible, resourceful and always ready to help. I love learning new things and sharing knowledge with others. In my spare time, I love sports, music and traveling. I am a hardworking and reliable person and I'm sure that these qualities make me ideal for every job.
